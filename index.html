Grafeas

An open artifact metadata API to audit and govern your software supply chain

Universal artifact metadata

Store, query, and retrieve critical metadata about all of your software artifacts, regardless of their type and where they are stored. Get 360-degree visibility across a variety of environments, including on-premises, private, and public cloud clusters.

Pluggable

Easily add new metadata types and providers as your software supply chain grows and evolves. Bring over metadata for analysis from different tools used across different stages of the software development lifecycle.

Strong access controls

Carefully control read/write access to critical metadata about your development pipelines. Set access controls depending on the role of metadata producers and consumers.
